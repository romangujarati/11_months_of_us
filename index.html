Eleven months, my Jaan, my light,
of getting every wrong thing right.
Of reading you like my favorite book,
and falling deeper with every look.
So here's to you, and here's to us,
to love that's quiet, without the fuss.
I'm nowhere close to being done,
eleven months down, a lifetime to come.

You taught me something, quietly too,
the difference between the old and new.
I don't just save the words you send,
I keep them, treasure them, till the end.
Saved is for storage, kept is for gold,
and your every word is mine to hold.
Every letter, poem, and little note,
lives somewhere close, near my throat.

Now here's a truth I must confess,
I tried to be funny, more or less.
Eleven months of my finest jokes,
and the only laughter... was from other folks.
You're the comedian, that title's yours,
you're the one my laughter adores.
So I've retired, I've lost the fight,
making you smile is my job, and that feels right.

My one and only flower, by far.

never needed petals or any of those.
Why would I ask for flowers, my dear,
when the prettiest one is standing right here?
